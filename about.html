About the Borneo Museum Sarawak

Welcome to the Borneo Museum Sarawak, where history, culture, and heritage come alive. Located in the heart of Sarawak, our museum serves as a gateway to understanding the rich traditions and vibrant stories of the Bornean people.

What We Offer

Exhibition Cinema

At the Sarawak Museum's Exhibition Cinema, experience Borneo’s history, culture, and nature through exciting films. Learn about the rainforest, local traditions, and Sarawak’s rich heritage in a fun and educational way. Come and discover Borneo on the big screen!

A Celebration of Diversity

Sarawak is home to over 27 ethnic groups, each with its own language, traditions, and history. The museum proudly celebrates this diversity, offering a space where all visitors can explore and appreciate the region's multicultural identity.

Guided Tour

We guide you through the rich history, culture, and biodiversity of Borneo at the Sarawak Museum. Discover ancient artifacts, traditional crafts, and the extraordinary natural heritage of the region. Our expert guides provide fascinating insights into Sarawak's unique heritage, ensuring a memorable and educational experience for every visitor. Let us bring the stories of Borneo to life on your journey through the Sarawak Museum!

Aesthetic Brochure

Discover the rich history, vibrant cultures, and stunning biodiversity of Borneo through our brochure. It offers detailed information about our exhibits, events, and collections, helping you plan your visit and make the most of your experience at the Sarawak Museum. Explore the wonders of Borneo with us!

Visit Us

Whether you're a history enthusiast, a student, or a curious traveler, the Borneo Museum Sarawak promises a journey of discovery. Join us as we uncover the stories of our ancestors, the marvels of traditional practices, and the essence of Borneo’s cultural tapestry.
