How can we help you?

Account & Billing

To buy tickets for the Borneo Museum Sarawak, visit our .accordion-body Select your preferred date and time, and proceed with the payment. Once the purchase is confirmed, you’ll receive a digital ticket via email.

Yes, we offer special discounts for students, seniors, and children under 12. Please bring a valid ID when purchasing tickets at the counter or upload a scanned copy when booking online to qualify for the discount.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), debit cards, and secure online payment gateways. Currently, we do not accept cash payments for online bookings.

Website Issues

If you’re experiencing issues accessing our virtual tour, ensure that your browser is up to date and that you have a stable internet connection. The tour works best on Google Chrome and Firefox.

If you forgot your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. An email with password reset instructions will be sent to your registered email address.

Please clear your browser cache and cookies, or try accessing the site using a different device or browser. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@bms.com.
